# The 30-Day Survival Sprint

---

If you do nothing else, do this.

---

## Days 1-3: Protect the Core

- Put your primary email in a password manager.
- Change its password to a long unique password.
- Add hardware-key or authenticator-based multi-factor authentication.
- Secure your password manager.
- Secure your phone carrier account.
- Print emergency recovery information.

---

## Days 4-7: Freeze and Alert

- Freeze your credit.
- Turn on banking alerts.
- Turn on brokerage alerts.
- Remove saved cards from old services.
- Close unused payment apps.
- Lock or lower limits on accounts you rarely use.

---

## Days 8-14: Delete the Past

- Close unused accounts.
- Delete old public profiles.
- Remove personal details from social media.
- Opt out of data brokers where possible.
- Delete old cloud files you do not need.
- Remove unused apps and browser extensions.

---

## Days 15-21: Separate and Backup

- Separate daily spending from savings.
- Separate work and personal accounts.
- Make offline backups of critical documents.
- Store a spare hardware key safely.
- Write a family verification phrase.
- Create a recovery binder.

---

## Days 22-30: Company and Surface Review

If you run a company, lead a team, or control systems:

- Inventory assets.
- Review admin access.
- Remove old users.
- Protect domain registrar accounts.
- Require stronger MFA.
- Test backups.
- Write an incident plan.
- Verify payment-change procedures.

Do not wait for a perfect security program.

Start removing easy paths in.

---

## Final Note From 2030

The people who survived were not paranoid.

They were early.

They understood that the internet had changed before the culture admitted it. They understood that personal data had become targeting material, identity had become infrastructure, and speed had become dangerous.

They did not disappear.

They reduced.

They separated.

They verified.

They slowed money down.

They kept backups.

They removed dead accounts.

They made themselves harder to rank, harder to impersonate, harder to drain, and harder to destroy.

The bots came for everyone.

They did not spend equal time on everyone.

Become the target that costs too much.

Start now.
