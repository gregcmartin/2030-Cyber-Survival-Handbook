# One-Page Checklist

[Home](../README.md) | [Previous](07-30-day-survival-sprint.md)

---

Use this as the fast audit.

- [ ] Use a password manager.
- [ ] Give every important account a unique password.
- [ ] Use hardware security keys for critical accounts.
- [ ] Replace SMS codes where possible.
- [ ] Secure your phone carrier account.
- [ ] Freeze your credit.
- [ ] Close unused financial accounts.
- [ ] Remove saved payment methods from old services.
- [ ] Separate spending money from long-term money.
- [ ] Turn on transaction alerts.
- [ ] Delete unused online accounts.
- [ ] Remove exposed personal information.
- [ ] Back up important documents offline.
- [ ] Print an emergency recovery plan.
- [ ] Use human verification for large money moves.
- [ ] Inventory company systems and vendors.
- [ ] Remove old employee and admin access.
- [ ] Protect backups from deletion.
- [ ] Write an incident response plan.
- [ ] Reduce your digital life until it is defensible.
